Data Handling

ClearFolio Review operates on the following principles:

  • Data minimization
  • Purpose limitation
  • Access logging
  • Time-bound retention

We do not:

  • Reuse data
  • Sell data
  • Train models on client data
  • Share documents with third parties
  • Store documents indefinitely
